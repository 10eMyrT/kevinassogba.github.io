The purpose of this project is to apply Geographical Information Systems (GIS) tools to provide the spatial relational schema between different type of city climate and geography and habitat layouts, and the spread of Influenza A & B in New York State. GIS has a broad application in healthcare and is used for purposes varying from disease hot-spot analysis, optimal healthcare facility location, to drugs dispatch routing. This project used GIS tools like “Definition query”, “Add data”, “Hillshade”, “layout”, “raster calculator”, and layers such as Elevation, Roads, wind speed, parcels.

Goal: Solution approach to United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 3: “Good health and well-being”.
