A couple of months ago, I started a journey to strengthen my leadership skills for carreer success. Part of a university program, it has been an excellent trip to dig my inner-self, examine my strengths, and evaluate my weaknesses, which I discovered are more than I thought. From the recognition of my DISC styles, to Emotional Intelligence, we discussed topics such as Personal Branding, Professionalism, learning to create lollipop moments, etc. Among other things which I have discovered, Personal branding is key to success in many areas, and I have decided to work on improving mine. Hope you would not mind following me on this journey to improve others perception of us, even without personally knowing us.

From this day onwards, I will prediodically post on a new subject, which I believe will interest you. You will find a list of the topics of my current posts on your left. Make sure to regularly visit my website to read more about what I do, get insights about some of the challenging issues developpers face both on their projects, and in their personal leadership development.
